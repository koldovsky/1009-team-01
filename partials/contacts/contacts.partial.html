Rules

Our Basic Rules

  1. Privacy rule. When placing an order, you can use the right to privacy by indicating anonymity in the comments of your order.
  2. Home delivery. The order is delivered to the recipient personally or to a trusted person upon agreement with the recipient or the customer
  3. Delivery to the workplace, office, or business center. Delivery is made only to the reception. If the recipient cannot go down to the checkpoint or is not at the address at the specified time, the administrator of the online store agrees with the customer.

What flowers do you work with?

We work with absolutely all flowers. Roses, lilies, peonies, tulips, etc. — there are no favorite or unloved flowers for us.

Do you create exotic bouquets?

Yes, we work with exotic flowers, but only if they are available in our warehouse. Check the availability of the flowers you need by contacting us.

Can I make an exclusive order?

Each bouquet we create is exclusive unless you want a number of identical compositions for your purposes.

Can you decorate a cafe with flowers?

Yes, we provide decoration services and can fill your venue with selected flowers and beautiful compositions.

Can I order a bouquet at night?

Sure. Our florists work 24/7. Still, you'll need to pay extra for the delivery.
